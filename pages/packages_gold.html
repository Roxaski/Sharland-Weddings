wedding venue

We'd like to introduce you to our 'White Cherry' venue, this venue's design is inspired by Japan's white cherry blossom's in all their glorious beauty.

This venue is able to accomodate up to 80 guests, it comes decorated in an all white motiff to match the traditional wedding feel of elegance.

Our venue is located in one of our regal and majestic event halls, the perfect place to host a wedding event.

Included in your Gold Package is our standard dining package.

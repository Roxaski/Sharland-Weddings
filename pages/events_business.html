business events

We offer a variety of spacious function rooms, with beautiful picturesque views

conference rooms

We have a variety of conference rooms on offer spanning both of our venue locations, in both of them we are able to accomodate anywhere between 50 to 300 guests

Both Crystal Manor and Serenity Lake conference rooms are very spacious and are incredibly popular with bigger business groups due to it's sheer size and incredible location within the welsh countryside

We also offer smaller sized conference rooms, perfect for small meetings. All of our venues offer a wide range of catering options, if your interested then you can read more about it below

catering

We have an extensive array of options to cater for all your needs, coffee and tea is included along with some complimentary biscuits, you can choose from a variety of bourbos, rich tea or custard creams

If you prefer something a little more substantial then worry not as we do indeed offer hot meals. However these need to be ordered a couple of weeks in advance, as we always strive to procure fresh ingredients

Catering for all your needs is something that we take alot of pride in, so we always ensure that we can meet all of your dietary needs

team building excercises

We provide you with not only incredible and fun exrcises but we make sure that we have a great variety of them on offer. Do keep in mind that these are subject to change depending on weather conditions, and on the location choose to attend

Some of the team building exercises we have on offer are, white water rafting, archery and kayaking

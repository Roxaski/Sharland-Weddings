christmas events

experience the magic of christmas

christmas dinner

We have a variety of function rooms on offer spanning both of our venue locations, in both of them we are able to accomodate anywhere between 50 to 300 guests, the perfect size for your christmas parties.

All venues across both of our locations offer incredible views and spacious rooms for you to host your events.

We have a special menu on offer for this festive time of your, if your interested get in touch and we can let you know what we have on offer.

christmas presents

We are happy to provide every christmas party with a pinewood tree, decorated from head to toe with a variety of beautiful christmas decorations.

There is wrapping paper and tags available to wrap your presents if that's something you desire, from the bright and colourful to the merry and festive.

We even throw in some of our little festive treats for you to unwrap with your friends and loved ones.

christmas treats

What a better way to celebrate christmas than with some delicious christmas treats, created by hand from our own patisserie chef.

While you enjoy the little treats we have to offer, why not wash it all down with some eggnog and for those cold winter nights why not try some delicious hot coco, filled to the brim with toasted marshmallows.

What better way to spend your christmas than at one of our incredible festive and family friendly venues.

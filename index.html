crystal manor

This is one of our wedding venues, located in the beautiful Welsh countryside, situtated just south of the quaint little town of Beddgelert. This manor is privately owned estate, which is home to many a venue ranging from weddings to business events

The manor is home to many a function room, ranging from a wide variety of sizes, it is able to accomodate a wide range of guests, having the capacity of around three hundred per event

Crystal Manor has won a variety of awards, one of which was being featured on Bridals best venues of 2024

serenity lake

Our next incredible venue is located on the gorgeous Lake Como, Italy. It is home to our privately owned Villa, which is home to our beautiful gardens which are surrounded by five acres of land, all of which are all accesible to our wonderful guests

The villa is able to accomodate around two hundred guests for it's indoor venues, and a futher hundred guests with our expansive gardens, which make for incredible events and weddings during the summer months

Boat rides are included in the bookings of this venue
